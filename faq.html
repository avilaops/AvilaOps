What's included in the Free Tier?
The Free Tier includes 1,000 API requests per month, 1 GB storage, 1 user, community support, and basic analytics. Perfect for testing and small projects. No credit card required.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades will apply at the start of your next billing cycle. Pro-rated credits are applied automatically.

Enterprise pricing starts at $2,999/month and is customized based on your needs. It includes unlimited requests, storage, users, 24/7 dedicated support, and custom SLA. Contact us at enterprise@avila.inc for a quote.
What's your refund policy?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 30 days of purchase for a full refund, no questions asked.
